Privacy Policy.

1. Who we are

UselesslyInteresting.com is a website dedicated to sharing fun, weird, and fascinating facts. We are based in the Czech Republic and operate under EU law including GDPR.

2. What data we collect

We collect anonymous usage data through Google Analytics 4. This includes pages visited, time spent on the site, browser type, and general location (country/city level). We do not collect your name, email, or any personally identifiable information unless you voluntarily provide it.

3. Cookies

We use cookies for Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website. You can accept or decline cookies using the banner displayed on your first visit. If you decline, no analytics data will be collected.

We also use localStorage (a browser storage mechanism) to save your cookie preferences and any reactions you leave on facts. This data stays on your device and is never sent to our servers.

4. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 to analyze website traffic. Google may process your data on servers outside the EU. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy. You can opt out of Google Analytics by using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

5. Your rights (GDPR)

As an EU resident, you have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal data we hold about you. Since we collect only anonymous analytics data, there is no personal data to access or delete. For any questions, contact us via Instagram.

6. Third-party links

Our website may contain links to external sites such as Instagram. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these sites.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

8. Contact
